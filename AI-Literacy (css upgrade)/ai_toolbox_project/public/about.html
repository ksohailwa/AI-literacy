About the AI Literacy Toolbox

Artificial Intelligence is becoming an essential part of our daily lives and workplaces. This AI Literacy Toolbox was created to help close the knowledge gap, empowering people not just to use AI, but to understand and critically engage with it.

Our mission is to provide accessible and practical AI literacy for students, educators, and anyone curious. The toolbox combines interactive learning modules with playful tools based on proven AI literacy frameworks.

Developed as a university project at the University of Duisburg-Essen, we researched frameworks, collected valuable tools, and built a platform for learning and collaboration.
