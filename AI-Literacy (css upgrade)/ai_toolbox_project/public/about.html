About the AI Toolbox Project

This website was developed as part of our Bachelor's project in the summer semester 2025 at the University of Duisburg-Essen.

Its purpose is to promote AI literacy among students, educators, and the general public.

Our toolbox includes games and educational tools designed to train various competencies based on well-known AI literacy frameworks.

The project is carried out by the students:

  • Khan Sohail
  • Kevin Flotow
  • Mohammadreza Zahedizadehgan
  • Rabia Karabulut
  • Tharani Uruthirakumar
  • Gousiha Rammohan

under the expert guidance of our professor: Irene Choutna

Together, we researched frameworks, collected useful tools, and created a platform where users can not only learn but also actively contribute.

If you have any questions, ideas or suggestions about AI Literacy, please do not hesitate to contact us. We are always at your disposal.
